About Lent4Dummies

Lent4Dummies is here to simplify everything about Lent for you. Whether you are unfamiliar with the traditions or looking for ways to observe Lent in a more meaningful way, this site is your guide. Explore the different sections to learn more!

What is Lent?

Lent is a 40-day period of prayer, fasting, and almsgiving that starts on Ash Wednesday and ends on Holy Thursday in order to get Christians ready for Easter.
